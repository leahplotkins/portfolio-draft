The Jack

Assignment: Re-imagine the tong, focusing on ergonomics
Approach:To create tongs that are inherently universal, beautiful and environmentally friendly. The Jack differs from other tongs by providing a unique visual and physical experience. Doubling as both cooking and serving tools, The Jack does not limit the user to a narrow set of functions or a singular way to interact with it, making it a "Jack of all trades".
What I learned: How to make a successful product using all the steps of the design process.

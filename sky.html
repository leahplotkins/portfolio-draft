Sky & Sea

Concept: Create an installation piece of my interpretation of an imaginary space.
Approach:I created my interpretation of where the sky and sea meet at the horizon line. To do this I created an 11ft w x13ft l x 4.5ft t inflatable structure. Inside of the bubble like structure is another pocket of air that inflates and deflates as you walk on it, simulating water and the rest the space of the bubble simulates the air.
What I learned: Trust your gut as an artist and don't let anyone scare you out of creating what you want to create. .

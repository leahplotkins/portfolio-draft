City Scapes

Assignment: Find and photograph unique perspectives in the city. Use the photos to create illustrations considering color as a communicator of space.
Approach:I imported the photos I took into Illustrator and painstakingly traced and colored them in Photoshop. I wanted to create distinct worlds and color pallets for each composition.
What I learned: How the underlying structure of everything we see is simple drawing perspectives.
